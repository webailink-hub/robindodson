A Legacy of Excellence

Known worldwide as "The Bankroll" for her fearless play in high-stakes matches, Robin Dodson is a living legend in the sport of billiards. A dominant force on the 9-ball circuit throughout the 1990s, she solidified her place in history with back-to-back World Championships.

Beyond her titles, Robin is a gifted educator. Renowned for her instructional work, including the seminal "Mastering the Jump Shot," she brings a physics-based approach to teaching, combined with deep psychological insight into the competitive mindset. Whether you are holding a cue for the first time or preparing for a national tournament, Robin's mentorship is transformative.
